## What is SaleDock Desktop POS?

SaleDock Desktop POS is a lightweight, reliable desktop application that runs completely locally on your computer. It empowers retailers to process sales, manage stock levels, handle repair jobs, track supplier dues, and monitor shifts without needing a constant internet connection. Your business data stays privately on your device.

### Who is it for?
* **Mobile Accessory & Phone Shops** (built-in IMEI/serial tracking)
* **Retail Counters & Boutique Outlets**
* **Repair & Accessory Businesses**
* **Small and Medium Businesses (SMBs)** looking for speed and simplicity

---

## Key Features

* **Lightning-Fast Billing**: Enter barcodes or select items to complete transactions in seconds. Prints receipts (80mm) and standard A4/A5 invoice PDFs.
* **Inventory Management**: Track stock levels with minimum stock warnings and reorder thresholds.
* **FIFO & Stock Batches**: Track purchase vs. sale prices per batch.
* **Below-Cost Protection**: Optional manager block to prevent selling items at a loss.
* **Bill Tabs & Held Bills**: Save active carts to resume them later.
* **Shift Closings & Cash Drawer**: Manage cash register float, log cash-in/cash-out, and generate daily closing reports.
* **Returns & Refunds**: Process full or partial returns cleanly with restocked stock updates.
* **Customer Accounts & Credit**: Maintain a ledger of customer balances and record credit payments.
* **Supplier Ledger & Dues**: Keep track of purchase orders, stock arrivals, and supplier payment history.
* **Cloud Backup Integration**: Option to import and merge your data with [SaleDock Cloud POS](https://saledock.site).
* **Backup & Restore**: Easily generate manual or automatic database backups to ensure safety.

## Quick Setup Instructions

### For Windows:
1. Download the `SaleDock-Desktop-POS-Windows-Portable.zip`.
2. Extract the ZIP to a folder on your computer (e.g., `C:\SaleDock`).
3. Double-click `SaleDockDesktopPOS.exe` to launch.
4. *Note: If Windows SmartScreen displays a warning, click **More Info** -> **Run anyway**.*

### For macOS:
1. Download the `SaleDock-Desktop-POS-macOS-Portable.zip`.
2. Unzip and drag `SaleDock Desktop POS.app` into your `Applications` folder.
3. If macOS states the app is "damaged" (quarantine flag):
   - Open Terminal and run:
     `xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine "/Applications/SaleDock Desktop POS.app"`
4. Right-click the app and choose **Open**.

## ⚠️ Safe Updates Note

Your shop data and database are stored locally, completely separate from the application folder:
* **Windows**: `%AppData%\GadgetZonePOS`
* **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/GadgetZonePOS`

Upgrading the application files will **not** delete your shop records. However, we highly recommend generating a safety backup from the POS Settings tab before updating.
